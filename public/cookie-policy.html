Cookie Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

GreenNest Garden uses cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, analyze traffic, and support advertising/analytics services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences and improve user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand site usage and performance.
  • Advertising Cookies: May be used by ad partners to serve relevant ads.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.

Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

You may request a return within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

Non-returnable items may include final sale products, gift cards, and items marked non-returnable at checkout.

Once your return is received and inspected, approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Start a return by contacting: returns@yourdomain.com
